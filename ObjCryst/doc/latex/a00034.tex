\subsubsection{Detailed Description}
Global Scattering Power. Used to approximate the scattering power of a multi-\/atom \doxyref{ZScatterer}{p.}{a00155} (polyhedron,...) to an isotropic scattering power.

The scattering power will be ({\bfseries slowly}) approximated to the average scattering power of this \doxyref{ZScatterer}{p.}{a00155} take in all orientations. The approximated scattering factor will include the temperature factor and resonant scattering factors contributions.

This is only used in \doxyref{ZScatterer}{p.}{a00155}, and maybe obsolete (?).
